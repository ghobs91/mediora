Mediora is a powerful media client that connects to your Jellyfin media server, allowing you to stream your personal media collection on your Apple TV.

KEY FEATURES:

• JELLYFIN INTEGRATION
Connect to your Jellyfin server and access your entire media library. Browse movies, TV shows, and more with a beautiful, native Apple TV interface.

• OPTIMIZED FOR APPLE TV
Experience your media with an interface designed specifically for the big screen. Navigate effortlessly using the Siri Remote with smooth animations and intuitive controls.

• SEAMLESS PLAYBACK
Enjoy high-quality video playback with support for various formats. Resume watching from where you left off across all your devices with continue watching support.

• SMART SEARCH
Quickly find what you want to watch with powerful search functionality across your entire media library.

• TMDB INTEGRATION
Get rich metadata, artwork, and information about your movies and TV shows powered by The Movie Database (TMDB).

• LIVE TV & EPG (IPTV)
Watch live TV channels and browse the electronic program guide directly on your Apple TV.

• SONARR & RADARR SUPPORT
Manage your media collection with Sonarr and Radarr integration. Add new shows and movies to your library right from the app.

• iCLOUD SYNC
Keep your settings and preferences synced across all your Apple devices with iCloud.

• PRIVACY FOCUSED
Your data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information or track your viewing habits.

REQUIREMENTS:
• A Jellyfin media server (visit jellyfin.org)
• Network access to your server (local or remote)

Note: Mediora is a client application only. You need to set up your own Jellyfin server with your personal media collection. Mediora does not provide any media content.
