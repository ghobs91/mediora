# Mediora

**Your Ultimate Media Center for Apple TV**

Mediora is an **open-source tvOS app** that transforms your Apple TV into a powerful, self-hosted media hub. It integrates seamlessly with **Jellyfin**, **Sonarr**, and **Radarr**, allowing you to browse, discover, request, and watch content—all from the comfort of your couch.

If you run a self-hosted media stack and use Apple TV, Mediora is built for you.

---

## Features

### Jellyfin Integration
- Stream your personal Jellyfin media library directly on Apple TV
- Browse movies, TV shows, and live TV
- Resume playback where you left off
- Full metadata support with rich artwork and descriptions

### Discover New Content
- Integrated TMDB (The Movie Database) search
- Explore trending movies and TV shows
- View detailed information, trailers, and cast details
- High-resolution posters and background artwork

### Content Management
- Request movies through **Radarr**
- Request TV shows through **Sonarr**
- Monitor download progress in real time
- Automatic library updates when content becomes available

### Live TV
- Watch free IPTV channels from around the world
- Browse channels by country and region

---

## Supported Services

- **Jellyfin** – Media streaming and library management  
- **Sonarr** – TV show requests and monitoring  
- **Radarr** – Movie requests and monitoring  
- **TMDB** – Content discovery and metadata
